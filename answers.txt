Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: To make sure that when multiple people are contributing to a 
single project, there is an efficient and safe way to streamline this 
process.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Medicine. Since there are so many medical datsets, datascience 
could be used to sort through and then make predictions based on previous 
data.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging is a safe option that preserves the entire history of your 
repository, while rebasing creates a linear history by moving your feature 
branch onto the tip of main.
